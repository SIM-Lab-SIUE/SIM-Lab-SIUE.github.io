Featured Work

The lab's major initiatives — spanning open education, qualitative research tooling, and production infrastructure for the department.

Open Science / OER

The Teaching Ecosystem

A unified open-education stack for undergraduate research methods at SIUE — a CC BY 4.0 textbook, a course delivery platform, and an R package that ties them together. Built to make methods instruction transparent, reproducible, and accessible.

From Vibes to Variables

A 22-chapter Quarto Book guiding undergrads from curiosity to a reproducible study using a real dataset of 1,792 songs. Licensed CC BY 4.0.

coursepackR

Qualitative Research

MethodoSync

A browser-based qualitative research tool for video annotation and codebook generation. Built with React, TypeScript, and Vite — paste transcripts or YouTube links, apply grounded codes, collapse to axial categories, and export structured Markdown or Excel codebooks. No server required; all processing happens in the browser.

Launch App
Production App

The Cage

The MassComm Checkout System — a full-stack production application for managing and reserving departmental production equipment at SIUE. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and PostgreSQL, featuring Microsoft Entra SSO authentication, QR-based checkout workflows, and a dual-database architecture with Prisma ORM.
