#introduction

SIM Lab @ SIUE

SOCIAL & INTERACTIVE MEDIA LAB @ SIUE

The SIM Lab @ SIUE is a means of collecting the work of Dr. Leith and his collaborators at SIUE under a single umbrela. The creation of the lab allows Leith and the other Mass Communications faculty to seek more diverse funding opoprtunities while still applying for project specific grants related to social and interactive media. This about page lists the work created since Leith was hired. In the future, any work completed under the umbrella of the SIM Lab @ SIUE, regardless of Leith's involvement, will also be included.

Github: @SIM-Lab-SIUE
